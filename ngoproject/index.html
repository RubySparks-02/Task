Education

Education is Essential for
Better Future

Our institution is committed to implementing a robust academic strategy aligned with the NEP 2020, focusing on “Access, Equity, Quality, and Accountability.” We prioritise holistic development and offer diverse optional subjects such as Fine Arts, Information Technology, Sports, Agriculture, and Vocational Training. Students also have opportunities to engage in various hobby clubs. Our Sainiki Vidyalaya is pivotal in fostering national pride and nurturing aspirations to serve in the esteemed Indian forces. We are dedicated to providing quality education and empowering underprivileged children, especially girls.
